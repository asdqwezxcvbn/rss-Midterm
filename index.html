About This Site

This website is a personal selection of RSS feeds from MIT that I find interesting. The reasons i chose each of these RSS feeds are as follows

  • Robotics - I was a part of my highschool's robotics team and ever sense then I have had a deep interest in anything and everything to do with robotics.
  • Neuroscience - I chose this RSS feed as I have immense interest in neuroscience's and I love topics which have things to do with neurology which is something i very heavily considered pursuing in college.
  • Science - I chose this RSS feed as i have always had a nack for science and i have always loved going into depth in research on science related topics as I have a love for all types of science.
  • History - Similar to science I chose history as ive always loved learning about all sorts of history, especially loving learning about the two world wars and other wars which included America and other global super powers.

The aim is to share these feeds in a user-friendly format, allowing you to quickly browse and find the information that I found compelling.
